Ginevra de' Benci Painting

Ginevra de' Benci is a portrait painting created by Leonardo da Vinci. It depicts the young Florentine aristocrat, Ginevra de' Benci. The painting is known for its meticulous detail, delicate expression, and the inclusion of a juniper branch, a symbol of fidelity. It is currently housed in the National Gallery of Art in Washington, D.C.

Mona Lisa Painting

The Mona Lisa is Leonardo da Vinci's most famous painting and an iconic masterpiece of the Italian Renaissance. It portrays a woman with an enigmatic smile, captivating viewers for centuries. The painting is celebrated for its exceptional technique, subtle use of color, and atmospheric perspective. It is displayed in the Louvre Museum in Paris.

Lady with an Ermine Painting
